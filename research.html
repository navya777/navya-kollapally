Research Overview

My research focuses on ethical artificial intelligence, clinical informatics, and ontology-driven NLP. I integrate multi-modal data with language models and Graph RAG reasoning to enhance fairness, interpretability, and transparency in health and education systems.

I am actively recruiting undergraduate and graduate students with strong Python skills who are interested in NLP, AI fairness, and health informatics. If you are passionate about these fields, consider joining my lab at Kean University.
